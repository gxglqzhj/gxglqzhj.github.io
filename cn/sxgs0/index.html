\(\int a^{x}dx=\dfrac{a^{x}}{\ln a}+C (a>0,a\neq 1)\) \(\int e^{x}dx=e^{x}+C\)
\(\int \sin xdx=-\cos x+C\) \(\int \cos xdx=\sin x+C\)
\(\int \tan xdx=-\ln \vert \cos x \vert +C\) \(\int \cot xdx=\ln \vert \sin x \vert +C\)
